Frequently Asked Questions

Are the tools free to use?

Yes, all IPToRoute tools are completely free for both personal and commercial use.

Is my data secure?

IPToRoute is a client-side application. All processing happens in your browser, and your IP addresses and network configurations are not transmitted to our servers.

Can I use IPToRoute offline?

Currently, IPToRoute requires an internet connection to load. However, once loaded, the core tools will function without further internet access.

How do I report a bug?

You can report bugs through our GitHub Issues page or by using the contact form on this page.
