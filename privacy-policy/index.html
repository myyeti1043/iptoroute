IPToRoute - Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2024

Introduction

Welcome to IPToRoute. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your personal data when you visit our website and tell you about your privacy rights.

Data We Collect

IPToRoute is a client-side application. The IP addresses and network configurations you enter are processed entirely in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers. We do not store your IP addresses or configurations.

However, we do collect the following information:

  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics that help us understand how users interact with our tool
  • Cookies: Small text files stored on your device to remember your preferences

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to collect anonymous data about how users interact with our site. This helps us improve our services and user experience. The information collected includes:

  • Pages visited
  • Time spent on site
  • Browser and device information
  • Referring websites

Google Analytics uses cookies to collect this information. You can opt out of Google Analytics by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Improve our website and services
  • Understand how users interact with our tools
  • Remember your preferences

Data Security

The security of your data is important to us. Since IPToRoute processes data client-side, your sensitive network information never leaves your browser. We implement appropriate security measures to protect the limited data we do collect.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure of your data
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using our contact form.

Changes to This Policy

We may update our privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through our contact page.
